## 💎 The Athlete's Toolkit

### 🏗️ Dynamic Cycle Architect
Stop planning week-by-week. Our **Cycle Designer** allows you to build multi-week training blocks with ease. 
- **Week Cloning**: Mirror your master week with one click.
- **Smart Progression**: Automated weight scaling based on your historical PRs.
- **Cycle Extension**: Seamlessly add 1, 2, or 4 weeks to a successful block.

### 📈 Surgical Analytics
Visualize your journey with pro-grade data viz.
- **Progressive Overload Tracking**: Monitor volume and intensity trends per muscle group.
- **Weight Correlation**: See how your body weight fluctuations impact your relative strength.
- **Automatic PR Logic**: Every lift is checked against your history to celebrate new milestones.

### 🛡️ Secure, Fast, Reliable
Built on a modern stack to ensure your data is always ready when you are.
- **Zero-Flash Auth**: Server-side proxy handling for seamless redirects.
- **Gym-Floor UX**: Minimalist, high-contrast logging interface designed for use between sets.
- **Automated Reports**: Comprehensive performance summaries delivered at the end of every cycle.

## 🗺️ Roadmap: The Future of Peak Performance

We are evolving TRAK.FIT from a tracker into an intelligent coach.

### 🌀 Phase 1: Intelligence Core (Q2 2026)
- **Wearable Biometrics**: Bi-directional sync with Apple Health, Oura, and Garmin to correlate recovery (HRV/Sleep) with gym performance.
- **RPE & RIR Integration**: Layer subjective effort data over objective tonnage for a complete fatigue profile.
- **PR Prediction**: proprietary algorithms to predict 1RM based on sub-maximal lift velocity and volume.

### 🏆 Phase 2: Mastery & Social (Q3 2026)
- **The Arena**: Opt-in leaderboards categorized by weight class and training style.
- **Verified PR Feed**: Share your biggest wins with the community via cryptographically verified session logs.
- **Coach-Link**: Allows personal trainers to design cycles remotely and monitor client progress in real-time.

### 🧠 Phase 3: Hyper-Personalization (Q4 2026)
- **AI Voice Logging**: Completely hands-free logging—just tell the app what you lifted.
- **Computer Vision Form Check**: Real-time analysis of your bar path and joint angles using your phone's camera.
- **Nutrition-Performance Correlation**: Tracking how specific macro-profiles impact your peak power output.

- **Storage:** [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/)
- **Aesthetics:** [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com/)
